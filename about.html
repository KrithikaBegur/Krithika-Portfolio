About Me

I'm a Business & Data Analyst who brings clarity to ambiguity, turning scattered requirements and vague questions into structured, data-backed solutions. I recently completed my MS in Management Information Systems from Arizona State University (4.0 GPA), specializing in Data, AI, and Cybersecurity.

Previously, I spent 3.5+ years at IBM working across Business Analysis and Data in the telecom domain, collaborating with engineering, QA, and product teams to define requirements, validate systems, and ensure production quality.

I enjoy work that sits at the intersection of requirements gathering, data modeling, dashboards, and cross-functional problem solving.
